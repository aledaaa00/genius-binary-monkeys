EARTHQUAKE

When the ground shakes...
🐢

DROP, COVER, AND HOLD ON!

Hide under a strong table and protect your head like a turtle!

🔥

WILDFIRE / FIRE

If you see smoke or fire...
🛑

GET LOW & GO!

Crawl on the floor where the air is clean. If your clothes catch fire: STOP, DROP, and ROLL!

🌊

FLOODS

When the water rises...
🧗

CLIMB HIGH!

Never play in flood water! It's dirty and moves fast. Go to the highest floor of the house.

🌪️

STORM / TORNADO

When the wind is super strong...
🏠

STAY AWAY FROM WINDOWS!

Go to the basement or an inside room with no windows. Stay with your family!
